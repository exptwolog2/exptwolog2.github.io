About us

Hello ! This is the official webpage of the d^2 (Doucet-Deligiannidis) group, a research group led by Arnaud Doucet and George Deligiannidis in the Department of Statistics at the University of Oxford.

Our research spans a number of topics in computational statistics and machine learning, including deep learning, Bayesian inference, generative modeling, learning theory, probability, optimization and Monte Carlo methods.
